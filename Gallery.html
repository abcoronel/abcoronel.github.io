I want to do my data analysis assignment on animals. I will be focusing on endangered species. I chose this Infographic! I think that this infographic is interesting because it really captives an audience by expressing awareness on the various endangered species: whales, polar bears, penguins, and loggerhead turtles. The data and diction of the infographic are also very carefully chosen. For instance, only 350 north american whales still exist or polar bears are 10% thinner. Firstly, 350 is such a small number of whales. Secondly, instead of saying the number of polar bears that are still alive, the author used a different statistic to really enhance the scarcity of polar bears. Lastly, the colors and visual aspect is really entertaining to read.
